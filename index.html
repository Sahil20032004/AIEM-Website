AIEM CLUB

When Ideas Meet Reality

#WhenIdeasMeetReality
Yes, you heard it right! We are the A.I.E.M. club at VIT Bhopal focussed for robotics, with the help of electronics, mechanics, computer vision and bio-technology. With participation in many events to building some resources for the betterment of the society, come be a part of this exhilarating journey.

Technical Departments

Modeling & simulation

This division will be mainly focusing on the CAD and CAE aspect of engineering. You will get hands on experience on making the physical component from scratch to reality.

Machine Learning & Computer Vision

Machine learning & computer vision is the important block of building a robot and other smart devices.We will conducting Webinar, Ideathon, Project Competition, Hackathon Training Programs and also Paid Machine Learning Competition.

Electronics

Get a practical experience on use of electrnoics in machine and in robots.We are also planning to work on Projects based on Arduino, Raspberry Pi and also we will be working on communication because our club is also related to the Cybernetics.

Bioengineering

Bioengineering is the application of engineering knowledge to the fields of medicine and biology it is a well grounded field of engineering which includes biology engineering knowledge that is broad, drawing upon electrical, chemical, mechanical, robotics and other engineering disciplines.

Software Development Cell

In this department will be focusing on development of our applications and website ,and maintaining it and updating it with the time, where students/members will get various information about the club. You will get live session on building website and also mobile application.

Non-Technical Departments

Media

This department will be focusing on running our all social media platform and also making content for it such as posters, videos etc. Students who are intrested in Photo Editing, Video Editing, Content Creation can join this department.

Documentation

This department will be handling all of the documentaion work such as blogs, captions, articles etc. Those student who have interest in blogging or writing can join this department.

Finance

This department will validate and manage all of our club's expenses whether we are using our funds in making any robot or any event. This departemnt will have all the records regarding the expenses.

Logistics And Sponsorship

This department will be focusing on alloting material and other things which are required in making any project. Also it will be focusing on towards bringing some sponsors. Those who have good communication skills can join this department.

Club Management Departments

Project Monitoring Team

This department will host and manage the complete AIEM's IdeaManch program. In which student will showcase their ideas which can help society and this department will select the best idea and provide all the required material and team to make IDEA INTO REALITY.

Admin Panel

This department will be focusing on running the club smoothly. Members in this club will be validating every decision of club and also responsible for approving any event, workshop etc. by any department of club.
